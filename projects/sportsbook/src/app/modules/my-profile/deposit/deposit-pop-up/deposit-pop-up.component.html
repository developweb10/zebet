GTCollections Deposit With GTBank Website
  1. Log in to your GTBank internet banking platform www.gtbank.com
  2. On the left-hand panel, click on “Payments & Collections”
  3. Click on “Sports and Gaming”
  4. Select ZEbet from the list of gaming operators.
  5. Click on “Make New Payment”
  6. Enter your ZEbet ID number (This is the number found at the top right of the home page just left to your wallet balance) and the desired deposit amount you wish to top up to your ZE-Wallet.
  7. Your ZE-Wallet will be credited immediately after the transaction is successful. For further enquires, kindly reach out to ze support at support@zebet.ng
  8. Minimum Deposit: ₦100
  9. Fee: ₦50
 Deposit With GTBank Mobile
  1. Login to the GTWorld on your mobile or handheld device then click on “Payments”
  2. On the next page, click on “Sports and Gaming”
  3. Select your bank account from the “From account” drop-down menu Select ZEbet from the “Category” drop-down menu then select ZEBET Channels from the “Product” drop-down menu. Click “Continue”
  4. Enter your ZEbet ID number (This is found at the top right of the home page when you click on your wallet balance), your telephone number and the desired deposit amount.
  5. The next page returns your ZEbet customer reference number, phone number and the name (as registered on your ZEbet account) basically for confirmation purpose.
  6. Enter your 4-digit GTBank transaction pin to finalize and click “Done”
  7. A confirmation of transaction page is displayed on the final screen and your ZEbet wallet is instantly funded.
  8. Minimum Deposit: ₦100

 Deposit With USSD
  1. Dial *737*50*Amount*451#
    Example: *737*50*100*451#
  2. Enter your registered phone number as your reference ID and click send.
  3. Enter your 737 pin or token code to complete your transaction and click Send. Your account is instantly credited.

  4. Enter the account number: 0589898338.
  5. Confirm the account name as "ZEGAMING NIGERIA LIMITED."
